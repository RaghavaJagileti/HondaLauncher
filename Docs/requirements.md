\# HondaLauncher MVP



\## Objective



Convert Samsung Galaxy Tab A7 Lite into a smart infotainment system for Honda City 2015.



\## Version 1 Features



\### Splash Screen



\* Honda logo

\* Startup animation

\* Load for 3 seconds



\### Dashboard



\* Navigation button

\* Music button

\* Camera button

\* Settings button



\### Navigation



\* Launch Google Maps



\### Music



\* Launch Spotify

\* Launch YouTube Music



\### Camera



\* Launch Android Camera



\### Settings



\* App configuration



\## Future Features



\### Version 2



\* Front parking camera

\* Dashcam recording



\### Version 3



\* OBD2 diagnostics

\* Vehicle health dashboard



\### Version 4



\* OTA updates

\* Voice assistant



\### Version 5



\* AI driving assistant

\* Trip analytics
